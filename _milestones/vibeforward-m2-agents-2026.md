Won 1st Place (Best All-Around Application) at vibeFORWARD: M2—Agents in New York with a team of five. We built FRTC — Fraud Ring Triage Copilot, a multi-agent system that autonomously hunts a coordinated fraud ring hidden in 90 days of bank transactions.

An unsupervised engine surfaces candidate rings; six specialist agents and an adversarial Skeptic corroborate or refute each one over Cognee shared memory; a synthesizer confirms the ring and streams the verdict to a live UI. It found the ring at 100% precision and recall, an exact match to the benchmark of $161,750.90 across 250 transfers.
