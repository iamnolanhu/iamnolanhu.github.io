## Hacking Agents Hackathon NYC: Winners Showcase

100 hackers. 20+ project submissions. 2 days of building. Incredible innovation.

Our first Hacking Agents Hackathon in NYC brought together developers to build AI agent solutions for real-world problems. The energy, creativity, and sheer determination we witnessed has left us absolutely inspired! For 24 straight hours, developers from everywhere gathered to push the boundaries of what's possible with AI. The results exceeded expectations.

### Winning Projects

#### 1st Place - The Reserve Drivers
**Team:** Rob Spectre, Carter Rabasa, and Melissa Herrera

Revolutionized Formula 1 race broadcasts with AI-powered direction for just 45 cents in token spend. Smart, efficient, and cost-effective.

#### 2nd Place - LeaseDaddy

Is your lease giving you the ick? This tool flags problematic lease clauses, compares agreements to thousands of others locally, and includes an AI hotline for practicing landlord negotiations.

#### 3rd Place - Klaus.AI

Parses board game rules and provides setup instructions plus strategic advice for your next turn.

### Special Categories
