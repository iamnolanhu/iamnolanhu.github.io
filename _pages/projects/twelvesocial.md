Transform lengthy videos into engaging social media clips using AI-powered analysis and automated generation.

## Overview

TwelveSocial leverages AI to automatically identify the most engaging moments in your long-form videos and transforms them into shareable social media clips. Perfect for content creators looking to maximize their reach across platforms.

## How It Works

1. **Upload** your video content
2. **Twelve Labs AI** analyzes visual, audio, and text
3. **Chat with AI** to describe the clips you want
4. **AI finds and creates** perfectly cut clips
5. **Download and share** on social platforms

## Features

- **Video Upload & Processing** - Seamless upload with Twelve Labs AI analysis
- **AI Chat Assistant** - Conversational interface powered by GPT-4o for finding perfect clips
- **Smart Video Search** - Visual, audio, and text-based search capabilities
- **Automated Clip Generation** - Creates social media-ready clips automatically
- **Multi-Platform Ready** - Optimized for Instagram, TikTok, YouTube Shorts, and more
